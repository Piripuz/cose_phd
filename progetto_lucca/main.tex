\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

Dear Admission Committee,

I am writing this Research Statement to express my interest in pursuing a PhD in Economics, Analytics and Decision Sciences at IMT Lucca.

Although applying for an Economics PhD, you can see from my CV that I don’t have any degree in Economics.
I indeed studied Mathematics for my bachelor, at Sapienza, and Autonomous Systems Engineering for my master’s, at DTU, Denmark.
During my Bachelor, I was mainly interested in theoretical mathematics as algebra and topology;
during my master I mainly focused in theoretical and applied computer science, machine learning and some control theory.

Having this unusual background, I did my Master's Thesis in Transport Economics at EPFL,
under the supervision of André de Palma and Nikolas Geroliminis,
and this approach with Economics convinced me to pursue a PhD in the field,
while slightly moving away from pure Transport Economics.
